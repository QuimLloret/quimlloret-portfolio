QUIM LLORET VENTURA

I was born in 1999 in Girona, Spain. I love spending quality time with my loved ones and I always try to do my best in my projects, work and relationships. One of my favourite quotes is the following.

STUDIES

Since I was little, my taste for video games and the media in general has grown. I went to the institute and did the scientific baccalaureate. Then I entered the university world where I graduated in Computer Engineering. Now we are a year later, where I am studying a Data Science Master at the same university.

WORK

My first ever job was working as an ice cream vendor in a coastal town called Estartit in Spain. There I learned what face-to-face customer service is and had contact with diverse cultures and various languages.

Later I worked in Girona as a summer monitor for primary school children. It was not only about monitoring their activities, meals, etc. They were also spoken to in English. In this way, I not only taught them the language, but also reinforced it for myself. It was a beautiful experience.

Then I focused on the university and did not work until 2022, where I joined the Rotimpres company as an intern. I currently work there part-time in their IT department. I develop programs mainly in Java for internal clients.
